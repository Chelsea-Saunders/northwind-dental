​We love to see patients of all ages at Northwind Dental, and we take special interest in making it fun for kids to go to the dentist. You can trust Dr. Dawson to take time to answer your questions, and give you honest and professional answers to any concerns you may have.

We understand that going to the dentist isn’t something that most people look forward to, so we are committed to making each visit as pleasant and worry-free as possible. Whether you’re in pain, interested in Invisalign, or are looking for someone who can help you improve your routine oral health, you can relax, knowing you’re in good hands. At Northwind Dental you can expect to be treated like family, because seeing you is the best part of our day!

If you’re looking for a remarkable dentist in Wasilla, who is gentle, compassionate and will listen to your concerns, you will find it with Dr. Dawson.
